Preview


Wasn't IPL about talent meeting opportunity, and all that jazz? If 13 seasons down the line that is what still moves you about the tournament, Sunrisers Hyderabad are the most exciting team to watch. Only four games down in the tournament, more out of lack of choice than willingness, they have had to rely heavily on their young brigade to come good for their prospects to shine. From Priyam Garg, Abhishek Sharma, Abdul Samad and T Natarajan, they have had to eke out every bit and piece of their cricketing skill to find themselves on an even keel with Mumbai Indians on the points table - with two wins in four games.
